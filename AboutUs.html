About Whiskasip

At Whiskasip, we believe every great day starts with a great cup of matcha. Guided by our tagline, "Your Go-To Matcha Dorm Fix," our journey began as a small student-run business serving freshly whisked matcha drinks within university dorms.

What started as a simple way to satisfy late-night cravings and fuel busy study sessions quickly grew through the support of fellow students who shared our love for authentic, high-quality matcha.

Over time, Whiskasip evolved from a humble dorm-side venture into a growing brand dedicated to bringing premium matcha to a wider community without losing the warmth and convenience that inspired us from the beginning.

To ensure every cup delivers exceptional flavor and quality, we source our ceremonial-grade matcha powder directly from our trusted supplier in Shizuoka, Japan, a region renowned for producing some of the world's finest matcha.

Every sip reflects our commitment to authenticity, freshness, and creating moments of comfort, one perfectly whisked cup at a time.

Our Purpose

MISSION

We strive to provide authentic matcha beverages and desserts made with premium ingredients while delivering exceptional customer experiences. Every cup is carefully prepared to bring comfort, happiness, and energy to students and matcha lovers.

VISION

Our vision is to become one of the most recognized matcha brands in the Philippines, known for quality, authenticity, affordability, and innovation while sharing the culture of Japanese matcha.

QUALITY YOU CAN TRUST

Every Whiskasip beverage is made using ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from Shizuoka, Japan. We carefully prepare every order to preserve the rich flavor, vibrant color, and smooth texture that authentic Japanese matcha is known for.

Our commitment goes beyond great drinks. We continuously improve our products, service, and customer experience while staying true to the passion that inspired WhiskaSip from the very beginning.
